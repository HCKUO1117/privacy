  • 提供與維持遊戲功能，包括讀取與保存遊戲進度。
  • 處理內購商品顯示、購買流程與購買結果通知。
  • 顯示與管理廣告內容，並衡量廣告服務表現。
  • 改進 APP 穩定性、除錯、偵測異常與提升使用體驗。

本 APP 目前不提供會員註冊、登入帳號或雲端同步功能。你在 APP 內的大部分資料將保存在你的裝置本機，而不會建立個人帳號資料庫。

本 APP 目前可能使用下列第三方服務，其各自的隱私權政策將另外適用：

第三方服務供應商可能依其政策蒐集、處理與保存資料，我們建議你一併查閱該等服務的官方隱私說明。

部分資料會保存在你的裝置本機儲存空間，以便保留遊戲進度與設定。若你希望刪除這些資料，可透過刪除 APP 的方式移除本機保存內容。由於本 APP 目前不提供帳號系統，刪除 APP 通常也代表移除本 APP 可控制範圍內的本地資料。我們會採取合理措施保護資料安全，但任何網路傳輸或電子儲存方式都無法保證絕對安全。

7. 未成年人使用

本 APP 的主要對象為一般使用者，並非專為兒童設計。若未成年人使用本 APP，建議由家長或監護人協助了解本政策內容與使用情形。

8. 你的權利

  • 你可以透過刪除 APP 或清除裝置本機資料，移除部分本地遊戲資料。
  • 你也可以依適用法律要求查詢、閱覽、更正或刪除我們可控範圍內的個人資料。
  • 若涉及第三方廣告、支付或平台資料，可能需直接向相關服務供應商提出請求。

9. 政策更新

我們可能因功能調整、法律要求或服務變更而更新本政策。更新後版本將公布於本頁，並以頁面所示生效日期為準。

  • Advertising and device-related information, such as device data, IP address, cookies, or similar technologies used for ad requests, which may be collected by third-party advertising providers.
  • Basic technical information, such as operating system, app version, network status, and information needed for troubleshooting.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain gameplay features, including reading and saving game progress.
  • To display in-app products, process purchases, and notify users about purchase results.
  • To display and manage advertisements and evaluate advertising performance.
  • To improve app stability, debug issues, detect abnormal activity, and enhance user experience.
  • To comply with applicable law, platform rules, and payment service requirements.

4. Accounts and Sign-In

This app currently does not provide member registration, account sign-in, or cloud sync. Most user data in the app is stored locally on the device rather than in a user account database.

Third-party service providers may collect, process, and store data under their own policies. We recommend reviewing their official privacy disclosures as well.

This app is intended for general users and is not specifically designed for children. If a minor uses this app, parents or guardians should help them understand this policy and supervise use where appropriate.

8. Your Rights

  • You may remove some locally stored gameplay data by deleting the app or clearing local app data on your device.
  • Where required by applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data under our control.
  • Requests involving advertising, payment, or platform-managed data may need to be directed to the relevant third-party provider.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time due to feature changes, legal requirements, or service updates. The latest version published on this page will control as of the listed effective date.
