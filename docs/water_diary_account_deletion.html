帳號刪除說明

最後更新日期：2026 年 3 月 27 日

「淨水日記」支援使用者自行在 App 內刪除帳號。您也可以透過聯絡信箱與我們聯繫，要求協助刪除帳號與相關資料。

1. 如何刪除帳號

  1. 登入「淨水日記」App。
  2. 前往「設定」。
  3. 點選「刪除帳號」。
  4. 確認後，系統會開始刪除您的帳號與相關資料。

若您無法進入 App，也可以來信至 bad.tech.service@gmail.com 提出刪除申請。

2. 刪除後會發生什麼事

當帳號刪除完成後，與該帳號直接綁定的資料通常會一併刪除或停止保留，包括但不限於：

  • 登入帳號與身份驗證資料
  • 淨水器資料
  • 濾芯階段資料
  • 更換紀錄
  • 共享成員關聯資料
  • 通知設定、語言與時區設定
  • 推播通知 token

刪除完成後，您將無法再使用原帳號登入，也無法復原已刪除的資料。

3. 可能保留的資料

在以下情況下，我們可能依法律義務、爭議處理、防止濫用、帳務紀錄、系統備份或資安需求，於合理期間內保留部分必要資料：

  • 訂閱或交易識別資訊
  • 必要的伺服器日誌與安全紀錄
  • 依法必須保留的資料

這些保留資料將僅於必要目的與法律允許範圍內使用。

4. 僅清除本機資料

若您只想清除裝置上的快取與本機資料，而不刪除雲端帳號，可在 App 內使用「清除資料」功能。此功能不會刪除您的雲端帳號。

5. 聯絡方式

若您對帳號刪除或資料處理有任何問題，請聯絡：
HC
bad.tech.service@gmail.com

Account Deletion

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Water Purifier Diary allows users to delete their account directly within the App. You may also contact us by email if you need assistance with account or data deletion.

1. How to delete your account

  1. Sign in to the Water Purifier Diary app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the action to start deleting your account and related data.

If you cannot access the App, you may request deletion by emailing bad.tech.service@gmail.com.

2. What happens after deletion

Once account deletion is completed, data directly associated with your account will generally be deleted or no longer retained, including but not limited to:

  • Account and authentication records
  • Purifier data
  • Filter stage data
  • Replacement records
  • Sharing relationship data
  • Notification, language, and time zone settings
  • Push notification tokens

After deletion, you will no longer be able to sign in with the same account, and deleted data cannot be restored.

3. Information that may be retained

We may retain limited data for a reasonable period where necessary for legal compliance, dispute handling, fraud prevention, billing records, backups, or security purposes, including:

  • Subscription or transaction identifiers
  • Necessary server logs and security records
  • Information required by law

Any retained data will only be used for the purposes required and within the limits permitted by law.

4. Local data clearing only

If you only want to remove cached and local device data without deleting your cloud account, you may use the Clear Data feature in the App. This does not delete your cloud account.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about account deletion or data handling, please contact:
HC
bad.tech.service@gmail.com
